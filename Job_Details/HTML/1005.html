Eligibility Criteria

Nursing Officer

10+2 with Physics, Chemistry and Biology.

4 year B.Sc. Nursing OR General Nursing with 2 year Post Basic B.Sc. Nursing with minimum 45% marks.

SC / ST / OBC candidates get 5% marks relaxation.

Live registration in Madhya Pradesh Nurses Registration Council is required.

Sister Tutor

10+2 with Physics, Chemistry and Biology.

B.Sc. Nursing OR Post Basic B.Sc. Nursing.

Experience: B.Sc. Nursing / P.B.B.Sc. Nursing with 1 year experience.

Live registration in Madhya Pradesh Nurses Registration Council is required.
